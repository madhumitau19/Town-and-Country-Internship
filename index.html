Marketing & Design Intern (Spring 2026)
Town and Country Skateboards
Part-Time • 5-10 hrs/week
About Town and Country Skateboards
Town and Country Skateboards is an independent skate shop based in Scotia, NY, serving the Upstate New York skate community with gear, culture, and local connection. As we work toward an upcoming rebrand and renewed marketing presence, we’re looking for a creative, motivated intern to support art, social media, and brand development projects for the Spring 2026 semester.
Position Overview
We are seeking a Marketing & Design Intern with strong creative instincts and an interest in branding,social media, and small-business marketing. This intern will play an important role in helping shape the shop’s upcoming rebrand, designing visuals, and assisting with advertising and social media strategy. The ideal candidate is an artist or designer who’s curious about business, marketing, and subculture environments, ideally with interest in skateboarding, streetwear, or cannabis culture. This position is primarily remote, with the option for occasional in-person discussions or working sessions with Jacob, the shop owner, on campus.
This is a paid opportunity, offering $15 per hour.
Responsibilities
  • Assist in the development and execution of Town and Country’s rebrand, including updated visual identity, style guides, and design assets.
  • Create content for social media platforms (Instagram, Facebook, etc.), including graphics, photo/video edits, and promotional materials.
  • Support the shop’s advertising and marketing initiatives, including campaigns, digital ads, and community outreach.
  • Contribute to the design and development of shop-branded gear (stickers, apparel, boards, lifestyle items).
  • Collaborate with the owner to brainstorm creative ideas that align with the shop’s mission and target audience.
  • Research emerging trends in youth culture, skateboarding, lifestyle branding, and cannabis culture as they relate to marketing direction.
Preferred Qualifications
  • Currently pursuing a Bachelor's or Master's degree at Parsons.
  • Strong artistic or design skills (illustration, typography, layout, or branding experience).
  • Proficiency in creative tools such as Adobe Creative Suite, Procreate, Canva, or similar platforms.
  • Familiarity with social media trends, content creation, and audience engagement.
  • Interest in skateboarding, design culture, youth subcultures, and/or cannabis is a plus.
  • Self-motivated, organized, and comfortable working independently with remote guidance.
Time Commitment & Compensation
  • 5-10 hours per week, with flexibility depending on project load.
  • Bi-weekly or monthly stipend provided. $15 per hour.
  • Mostly remote work and in-person check-ins with Jacob on campus.
How to Apply
Interested candidates should send their resume, portfolio or design samples, and any relevant social media work to jacob@townandcountryskate.com.
